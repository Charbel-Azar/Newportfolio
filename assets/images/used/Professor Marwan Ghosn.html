Professor Marwan Ghosn's approach to oncology is rooted in a strong belief that each patient's journey is unique. With over three decades of experience, he has cultivated a practice that goes beyond only treatment to embrace the whole person.

"In oncology, we're not just fighting a disease; we're supporting a whole person’s needs on a journey," Professor Ghosn often says.

His philosophy is built on three pillars:

01 Precision Medicine

Leveraging cutting-edge genomic technologies to deliver personalized treatments tailored to each patient’s unique profile.

02 Holistic Care

Addressing not just the physical aspects of cancer, but also the emotional, psychological, and lifestyle factors that influence healing.

03 Multidisciplinary Collaboration

Orchestrating a team of medical expertise, where specialists from various fields harmonize their skills for optimal patient outcomes.

Academic Excellence

Professor Marwan Ghosn holds dual Doctor of Medicine degrees from Saint Joseph University of Beirut and the Republic of France. His extensive expertise in medicine is further supported by numerous postgraduate certifications in oncology and healthcare management, earned from prestigious institutions across Europe and the USA. His educational journey is enriched with Master’s degrees in Hospital and Health Management from the University of Paris 7 Denis Diderot and the Superior School of Business in Beirut, establishing him as a distinguished expert in his field.

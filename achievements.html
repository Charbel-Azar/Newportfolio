Achievements and Leadership

Throughout his career, Professor Ghosn has held numerous prestigious positions:

Academic and Hospital Leadership

  • - Professor and Chairman of the Department of Hematology Oncology at Hôtel-Dieu de France University Hospital
  • - Director of the Post-Graduate Program in Hematology Oncology, Faculty of Medicine, Saint Joseph University of Beirut
  • - Chairman of the Hematology Oncology Department, Faculty of Medicine, Saint Joseph University of Beirut
  • - Director of the Cancer Center at Clemenceau Medical Center- affiliated with Johns Hopkins International in Beirut
  • - Head of Hematology Oncology Division at Clemenceau Medical Center affiliated with Johns Hopkins International in Beirut
  • - Founder of In Good Health (IGH) multidisciplinary clinic in Beirut

Professional Organizations

  • - Acting Board Member for many years in the Lebanese Cancer Society (LCS)
  • - Co-founded the Lebanese Cancer Research Group (CRG)
  • - Served as President of the Lebanese Society of Medical Oncology (LSMO)
  • - Active Board Member in the Arab Medical Association Against Cancer (AMAAC)
  • - Editor-in-Chief of the Pan Arab Journal of Oncology (PAJO) for many years
  • - Co-founder and General Secretary of Africa MiddleEast Cancer Intergroup (AMCI)
  • - General Secretary of the Alumni of Institute Gustave Roussy Cancer Center in Villejuif, France

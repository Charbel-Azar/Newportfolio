Holistic and Multidisciplinary Approach

Professor Ghosn's expertise extends beyond clinical oncology to healthcare administration through a master's degree in hospital management and patient safety. As the founder of IGH (In Good Health), he has pioneered a comprehensive healthcare model that transcends traditional medical boundaries. IGH stands at the forefront of integrative healthcare, offering a comprehensive fusion of preventive medicine, holistic wellness, and cutting-edge treatments.

Under his leadership, IGH has established itself as a pioneer in:

Multidisciplinary Care Teams

Assembling comprehensive medical teams that include not just oncologists, but also other medical specialists alongside nutritionists, psychologists, and complementary medicine practitioners.

Lifestyle Integration

Emphasizing the role of nutrition, exercise, and emotional and mental wellbeing in cancer treatment and recovery.

Integrating Genomics

Professor Ghosn has been at the forefront of incorporating genomic testing into routine cancer care, ensuring that patients receive treatments precisely matched to their tumor's genetic profile.
